💡 How to Study With Music
Step 1 – Listen first: Play the song without looking at lyrics. Try to catch words you know.
Step 2 – Read along: Search "song name lyrics" on Google and follow the text while listening.
Step 3 – Highlight unknowns: Circle or note words/phrases you don't understand.
Step 4 – Translate & study: Look up those words and write them in a notebook with the Portuguese meaning.
Step 5 – Sing along! Repeat the song trying to match the singer's rhythm and pronunciation. This is called shadowing.
🎯
Teacher Ale's tip: Pick ONE song per week. Listen to it every day, memorize the chorus, and try to explain in English what the song is about. This alone will improve your listening dramatically!
